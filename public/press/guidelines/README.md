# WLPL Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview

Weight Loss Projection Lab (WLPL) is a HIPAA-compliant AI health platform. Our brand represents trust, innovation, and accessibility in digital health.

## Primary Brand Colors

### Digital RGB Values
- **Primary Blue**: `#4F46E5` (rgb(79, 70, 229))
- **Purple Accent**: `#9333EA` (rgb(147, 51, 234))
- **Success Green**: `#10B981` (rgb(16, 185, 129))
- **Warning Yellow**: `#F59E0B` (rgb(245, 158, 11))
- **Error Red**: `#EF4444` (rgb(239, 68, 68))

### Neutral Colors
- **Gray 900 (Text)**: `#111827` (rgb(17, 24, 39))
- **Gray 600 (Secondary Text)**: `#4B5563` (rgb(75, 85, 99))
- **Gray 200 (Borders)**: `#E5E7EB` (rgb(229, 231, 235))
- **Gray 50 (Background)**: `#F9FAFB` (rgb(249, 250, 251))
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` (rgb(255, 255, 255))

## Typography

### Primary Font
- **Family**: Inter, system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif
- **Weights**: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semibold), 700 (Bold)

### Headings
- H1: 3rem (48px), font-weight: 700
- H2: 2.25rem (36px), font-weight: 700
- H3: 1.875rem (30px), font-weight: 600
- H4: 1.5rem (24px), font-weight: 600

### Body Text
- Large: 1.125rem (18px), font-weight: 400
- Normal: 1rem (16px), font-weight: 400
- Small: 0.875rem (14px), font-weight: 400

## Logo Usage

See `/press/logos/` for logo files and usage guidelines.

## Voice & Tone

- **Professional**: Medical accuracy and HIPAA compliance
- **Empathetic**: Understanding family health challenges
- **Innovative**: AI-powered, cutting-edge technology
- **Accessible**: Easy to understand, jargon-free

## Imagery

- High-quality, authentic photos
- Diverse representation of families and individuals
- Focus on health, wellness, and technology
- Avoid stock photos that look overly staged
